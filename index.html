General Overview

Life Line is a health assistance and emergency support application. The app collects limited personal and health-related details to provide its services.

Information We Collect

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Blood Group
  • Location (GPS)
  • Health Condition / Medical History

Purpose of Collection

These details are used to create and manage your account, locate nearby emergency services and donors, provide real-time assistance during emergencies, and to send important service-related communications.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain personal information only while your account is active or as required to provide services. You may request deletion of your data through the app or by contacting support.

Data Sharing (Critical)

To provide urgent medical assistance, Life Line may securely share necessary information with verified third parties such as hospitals, ambulance providers, and emergency responders. Sharing is limited to what is strictly required to deliver immediate help.

Sensitive Data: Health & Location (Most Important)

Your health information and precise location are classified as sensitive personal data. We require explicit, informed consent to process these categories of information. This static page is informational; the live app requests consent before collecting or using sensitive data.

Key points:

  • Consent must be explicit (separate from general acceptance).
  • Consent should be freely given, specific, informed, and revocable.
  • You have the right to withdraw consent and request deletion of your sensitive data.
